AI-generated code quality risks. Design must be clean, professional, and 
mobile-first optimized.

- Primary Color: Deep Navy Blue (#1A365D) - trust and professionalism

- Text: Dark Slate (#2D3748) for body, Near Black (#1A202C) for headings
- White space: Generous padding, breathable layout
- Typography: Inter or SF Pro Display - clean, readable sans-serif
- Border Radius: 8px for cards, 4px for buttons
- Shadows: Subtle, layered (elevation 2-4)
